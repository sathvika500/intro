NARENDRA MODIJI

Early Life and Rise in Politics: Born on September 17, 1950, Modi began his political journey in the Rashtriya Swayamsevak Sangh (RSS) and later joined the Bharatiya Janata Party (BJP), where he held various organizational positions.
Chief Minister of Gujarat: He served as the Chief Minister of Gujarat from 2001 to 2014, implementing developmental projects and policies that aimed at economic growth and infrastructure development.
Becoming Prime Minister: Modi led the BJP to a significant victory in the 2014 general elections, becoming India's 14th Prime Minister, emphasizing economic initiatives and social programs.
: He secured a second term in the 2019 elections, continuing his leadership with policies focused on economic growth, diplomacy, and social initiatives.
